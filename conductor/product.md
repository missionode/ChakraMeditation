# Product Definition: Chakra Energy

## Initial Concept
This app is designed for me for my spiritual journey i will be using it for my self meditation and consultation.

## Vision
Chakra Energy is a personal spiritual companion designed to facilitate a deep, self-guided meditation experience and support spiritual consultations. It transforms the meditation practice into a guided, serialized journey through the seven chakras.

## Target Audience
- **Primary User:** The creator, for personal spiritual growth and daily meditation practices.
- **Consultation Context:** Used as a tool during spiritual consultations to provide visual and auditory aids.

## Core Features
- **Serialized Meditation Experience:** A guided, step-by-step session that automates the transition between chakras.
    - **Audio Guidance:** The app reads out the Chakra Name, Mantra, Location, Power, Element, and Description to prepare the user.
    - **Automated Session Flow:** After guidance, the specific chakra timer starts automatically with corresponding vibrational music.
    - **Auditory Cues:** Uses a bell sound to signal the end of a timer before proceeding to the next chakra in the sequence.
- **Vibrational Healing & Sound Therapy:** High-quality audio assets (Aum, Lam, Vam, Ram, Yam, Ham, Om) for specific chakra resonance.
- **Chakra Meditation Suite:** Dedicated visual and information cards for each of the seven primary chakras.
- **PWA Accessibility:** Fully responsive Progressive Web App with offline support.

## Design Philosophy
- **Serenity & Minimalism:** A clean, focused interface that promotes a meditative state.
- **Guided Flow:** A serialized experience that removes manual intervention, allowing for uninterrupted mindfulness.
- **Visual & Auditory Synergy:** Using traditional symbols, colors, and specific frequencies to create a cohesive environment.
