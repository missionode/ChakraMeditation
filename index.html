How to Meditate in Easy Steps

How to Meditate [Solo]

Sit comfortably and focus on your breath. Bring awareness to your body and any sensations you feel. You can also visualize yourself as a sacred space or object.

How to Meditate [Duet]

Sit facing your partner and gaze into each other's eyes. Hold hands or touch each other gently. Breathe together and focus on the energy between you.
